Introductions.

Macy Stephens is a Senior at the University of Colorado Boulder pursing a B.S. in Computer Science Engineering. She is a active member of the National Black Society of Engineers. Her goal to work on a team to develop new and exciting solutions to meaningful issues. She values different perspectives and strives to leave a positive impact on the world. Over her college career, she has gained experience in leadership, collaboration, and problem solving. She has developed apps and products as part of her classes. She has the ability to lead teams in bringing ideas together along with being a great partner and listener. One of the biggest impacts was when she and her team were able to provide a local leader in the theater industry a working website for them to further their business promoting reusable goods exchange. Her senior capstone is focused on entrepreneurship with launching a business. She is willing to get her hands dirty and learn as much as she can as long as she can see the impacts of her work. She strives to become a founder in a startup that will change the world. She wants to build a foundation for more diversity and inclusion in the entrepreneurial space.
